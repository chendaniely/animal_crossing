In New Horizons, the total number of fish is 80, up from 72 in New Leaf. New fish include the mahi-mahi, ranchu goldfish, snapping turtle, tilapia, betta, golden trout, rainbowfish, sturgeon, anchovy, suckerfish, and the barreleye. Dropped fish include Barbel steed, Eel, and Rainbow Trout.

River (Mouth): Fish with the River (Mouth) location can be found in the area where the ocean meets the river on your island’s shoreline.

River (Clifftop): Fish with the River (Clifftop) location can be found in rivers on elevated cliffs areas of your island.

Note: It is thought that rain and snow increase the chance of rarer fish to appear.

To view by month, go to the monthly fish list for New Horizons.
